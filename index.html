About Us

佛大CSA是一个以非营利为目的的学生组织，主要由中国留学生组成，并且服务于广大UF留学生以及推广中国文化的影响。

Chinese Student Association (CSA) at the University of Florida is an official non-profit, non-political student organization. It is a sub-organization under the Volunteers for International Student Affairs (VISA), funded by University of Florida Student Government.
